Artifical Intelligence

Definition:Artifical Intelligence refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and act like humans.These machines can learn, solve problems, make decisons, and adapt to new information.AI is used in wide range of applications from virtual assistants to self-driving cars.

Key Areas of Artifical Intelligence

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics

Real-World Applications

AI is used in voice assistants, Self-driving cars, recommendation systems, fraud detection, and many more.
